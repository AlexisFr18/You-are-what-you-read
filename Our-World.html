Let's add a little danger and adventure to our regular day lives.

Nothing is different in this realm, your still on earth living day by day. Only it's not your regular lifestyle, in this world you can be a young girl raised in a family full of famous thieves. Or a seventeen year old girl who joins an FBI program because of her skills to read the tiniest details of a person. Or maybe you can choose to be a woman that restores grave, only to have murder victims appear above your grave stones. If you're not interested in staying in the non- magical realm and truly wish for more excitement, I suggest the realm with a little more magic.
