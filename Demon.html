You are in the mountain city of Fellsmarch, and a reformed thief that goes by the name Hans Alister and will do anything to protect your mother and little sister, Mari. One day you take a powerful amulet from Micah Bayar, the son of the High Wizard. The amulet once belonged to the Demon King, the wizard who nearly destroyed the world a millennium ago. With a magical piece so powerful at stake, the Bayars will stop at nothing to reclaim if from you.
