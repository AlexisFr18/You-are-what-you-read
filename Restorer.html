The Restorer by Amanda Stevens

Home Page

Your name is Amelia Gray. You're a cemetery restorer who sees ghosts. In order to protect yourself from the parasitic nature of the dead, you've always held fast to the rules passed down from your father. But now a haunted police detective has entered your world and everything is changing, including the rules that have always kept you've safe. It started with the discovery of a young woman's brutalized body in an old Charleston graveyard you've been hired to restore. The clues to the killer, and to his other victims, lie in the headstone symbolism that only you can interpret.
